Recommended for

Those seeking a challenge, one requiring a lot of patience, focus, thought and sleigh of hand. This is a

game where gear alone won't carry you throughout the game, it'll only change how you play and how long

encounters last. While going in blind is the intended experience, DARK SOULS 3 features a plethora of

secrets that without guidance, would be almost impossible to find and figure out alone. Storytelling is

told through the environment, interaction and NPCs instead of the usual cutscenes.
